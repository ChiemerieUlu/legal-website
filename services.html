Frequently Asked Questions

Our fees vary depending on the type of case and complexity. We offer free initial consultations for most practice areas. Many personal injury cases are handled on a contingency fee basis, meaning you pay nothing unless we win your case. For other matters, we offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

The timeline varies significantly based on the type of case and its complexity. Simple matters may be resolved in weeks, while complex litigation can take months or years. During your consultation, we'll provide a realistic timeline based on your specific situation.

While we prefer in-person meetings when possible, we understand this isn't always convenient. We offer phone and video consultations for initial meetings and can conduct much of our work remotely. For certain matters like document signing, an office visit may be necessary.

Bring any relevant documents related to your case, such as contracts, correspondence, police reports, medical records, or court papers. Also bring a list of questions you'd like to ask. Don't worry if you don't have everything - we can work with what you have and request additional documents as needed.

Ethical attorneys cannot guarantee specific outcomes, as every case depends on unique facts and circumstances. However, we can promise to provide skilled, dedicated representation and fight vigorously for the best possible result in your case.

Simply contact us by phone, email, or through our website contact form to schedule a free consultation. We'll discuss your legal matter, answer your questions, and explain how we can help. There's no obligation, and all consultations are confidential.
